At Alfam Consumer Credit, I worked on a customer portal that let users manage their loans, review details, and handle common self-service interactions.

During the assignment I helped migrate the platform from .NET Framework 4.8 to .NET Core while continuing to deliver new functionality on the existing portal.

Alongside product and implementation work, I regularly acted as a security specialist for the team.
