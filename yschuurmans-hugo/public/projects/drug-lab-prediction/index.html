Q3 2018

Predicting Potential Drug Lab Locations

A machine learning initiative commissioned by Noord-Brabant province to forecast illicit laboratory sites.

sss Commissioned by the province of Noord-Brabant, this project explored whether geographic, demographic, and historical-incident data could be combined to predict where illicit drug laboratories were most likely to appear next.

Working in a small team during my Applied Data Science minor, I helped collect and clean dozens of public datasets, engineered features around isolation, road accessibility, and prior incident clusters, and trained gradient-boosted classifiers to score every 500×500 m grid cell in the province.

The deliverable was an interactive heatmap and a written report handed to the regional task force; the model surfaced several unexpected hotspots that aligned with later real-world findings.
